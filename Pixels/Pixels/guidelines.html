In the LowRes Coder community you can share programs, discuss, learn and have fun. Please follow these rules:

Discussion

  • Be nice and respectful with everybody.
  • There is no place for racism and gender discrimination.
  • Keep comments relevant to the topic. Don't spam, don't ask for likes and followers.
  • You may delete comments in your own posts which break with these guidelines. In other posts you can report them.

Posting Programs

  • All posted programs are free to adapt and share. You may not use them for commercial purposes.
  • Be creative! Don't just post someone's program without any own changes.
  • Give credit to the authors of the original code (in the source code or in the post description).
