Program your own retro games or demos directly on your iPad or iPhone!

- Run your programs on a virtual game console with 64×64 pixels, 16 colors, 8×8-pixel sprites and 3 sound generators.
- Write in a dialect of the classic, easy to learn BASIC programming language.
- Play the included games “Galaxy”, “Racing”, “Tennis” and “Snake”. Check how they are made and improve them with your own ideas.
- Send the source code of your programs to friends and publish them on the LowRes Coder website.
- Try games and demos made by the community.
